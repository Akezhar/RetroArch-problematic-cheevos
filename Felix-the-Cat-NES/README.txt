Felix the Cat (NES) 
===================

The Achievements for "Enter Bonus Area" don't work in RetroArch, but work fine in RANES (oficial emulator).

Description of the states:

state: above the bonus area on stage 1-1 [ http://retroachievements.org/Achievement/19874 ]

state1: above the bonus area on stage 1-2 [ http://retroachievements.org/Achievement/19875 ]

state2: above the bonus stage on stage 1-3 [ http://retroachievements.org/Achievement/19876 ]
